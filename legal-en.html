Returns & Refunds

You may return any item within 14 calendar days of receiving it, for any reason, in accordance with EU consumer law (Directive 2011/83/EU).

  • Email us at rodrigues.luisas@gmail.com within 14 days of delivery, stating your order number and intent to return.
  • We will reply with return instructions within 2 business days.
  • Return the item in its original condition and packaging.
